Cluster

The visual system for Cluster — a client investment portal and double-entry ledger. Built to feel like a serious, warm place to keep your money.

01

The mark

The wordmark sets cluster in a single weight beside a radiating sun — growth, returns, a new day for the saver. Keep the lockup intact; the sun never drifts from the word.

Primary · on ink
on surface
Symbol · app icon / favicon
Symbol · on coffee

Clear space

Keep free space equal to the height of the sun on all sides. Nothing crowds the lockup.

Minimum size

Wordmark no smaller than 96px wide on screen. Symbol no smaller than 24px.

Backgrounds

Use on ink, surface, white or coffee. Always keep strong contrast — never orange on orange.

✕Don't stretch or distort
✕Don't recolour the mark
✕Don't add shadows or effects
✕Don't place on clashing colour

04

Principles

01
Spend orange carefully

One clear action per view. Orange marks where to go next — overuse and it stops meaning anything.

02
Green and red are sacred

Used only for ledger credit and debit. Never for buttons, tags or mood.

03
Let the numbers align

All figures in tabular mono, right-aligned. A statement should scan like a ledger, not prose.

04
Warm, not casual

Coffee and surface keep things human; ink and discipline keep them trustworthy. Plain words, active voice.

05
Legible above all

Strong contrast everywhere, visible focus states, readable at small sizes. Trust is built in the details.

06
The mark stays whole

Word and sun travel together, unaltered. The symbol alone is for app icons and favicons only.
